Project Title

The wayfinding in DAAP has been stated to be flawed. Some of the problem areas include corners, where multiple signs are posted, unlabeled entrances, the naming of staircases, elevators, and areas of DAAP, and the directories that are placed at entrances. Overall, the wayfinding fails to direct newcomers or long-time students to necessary locations such as faculty offices, school offices, the CGC, the cafe, the photo lab, the woodshop, and the DAAPstore.
